# Plan: Sharpen Thesis (Option C)

## Core Framing

**The distinction to establish:**
1. The film's thesis: Fulfillment is exposure, not relief
2. The essay's argument: This thesis represents a *convergence* across traditions that share no common metaphysics

**The essay's contribution:** Not "applying frameworks to a film" but demonstrating that disparate traditions independently diagnosed the same structure.

**Convergence summary (¶4):**
> "These frameworks disagree profoundly on ontology, remedy, and hope. Yet they converge on the structure: delivery, not deprivation, is the wound."
